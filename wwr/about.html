History

Founded in 2002, White Water Rafting (WWR) began as a small group of passionate adventurers offering weekend trips down the Snake River. Over the years, we've grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region. With a commitment to safety, fun, and unforgettable experiences, we've guided thousands of thrill-seekers through some of the wildest rapids in the country.
